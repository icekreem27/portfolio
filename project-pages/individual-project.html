Third Year Project
Enhancing University Learning with Retrieval-Augmented Generation and GPT-3.5 Fine-Tuning
Project Details / Background
This study aims to investigate the potential of integrating advanced artificial intelligence models, specifically GPT-3.5, to enhance student support in university modules. The primary goal is to improve the chatbot using retrieval augmented generation and fine-tuning techniques to handle queries related to specific academic content effectively.
